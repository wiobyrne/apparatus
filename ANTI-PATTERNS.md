# Anti-Patterns

What Apparatus is not:

- Not glassmorphism.
  - No frosted cards, no translucent dashboard panels, no blur for its own sake.

- Not gradient hero sections.
  - Color is for meaning and orientation, not spectacle.

- Not card-based dashboards.
  - Readers should move through pages, not through a grid of equal visual objects.

- Not pill-heavy UI chrome.
  - Rounded chips and soft badges blur the line between navigation and decoration.

- Not infinitely customizable.
  - Apparatus is a posture, not a playground.

- Not “modern” by default web standards.
  - The default web aesthetic often optimizes for novelty and engagement, not reading.

- Not a component library first.
  - The system is about reading affordances, not shipping reusable widgets for their own sake.

- Not multi-column at the top of landing pages unless the content genuinely requires it.
  - Most introductions should be vertical and sequential.
